Work published at [L4DC 2023](http://l4dc.seas.upenn.edu/) with Vivek Borkar and Konstantin Avrachenkov.

Formulated and Implemented a new Deep Reinforcement Learning Algorithm based on [Full Gradient DQN](https://arxiv.org/abs/2103.05981) for average reward criterion. Extended the average reward RL Algorithm for Whittle Index Learning in Restless Multi-Armed Bandits.
