Well, I wanted a US B1 Visa to present my _first_ paper at a conference. Apparently, in India, the waiting time for an appointment is too much. So, I thought of getting one in France during the interval of my ongoing internship. It turns out there is no appointment available in France. 

However, there was one in Croatia. Thanks to the Schengen Visa, I went to Croatia, traveling multiple countries (Italy, Austria, and Slovenia). An IITB Alumnus helped me arrange a stay for free, and I got my Visa stamped in a day :)!

It was an adventurous experience for me, traveling 15 hrs by bus, aah!
